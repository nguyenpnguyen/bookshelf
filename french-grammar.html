Easy Learning: French Grammar

Collins

Easy Learning: French Grammar is a book that provides a clear and easy-to-understand guide to the verbs and grammar of French for beginners and intermediate learners. The book explains the essential grammar points using simple language and thousands of examples of real French. The book also covers the most common verbs and their conjugations, as well as the vocabulary needed for everyday situations. The book is designed to be accessible and practical, with key points, exercises, tips, and summaries throughout. The book is suitable for anyone who wants to learn French at school, at work, or at home, as well as for revision and reference purposes.

Language: English, French
