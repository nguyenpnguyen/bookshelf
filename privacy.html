Privacy Policy

At Bookshelf, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit our website, order books from us, or contact us. Please read this policy carefully and contact us if you have any questions.

Information we collect

  • Personal information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, billing address, and payment details. We collect this information when you create an account, place an order, or contact us.
  • Usage information: This includes information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit, the books you view or buy, the search terms you enter, and the device and browser you use. We collect this information using cookies and other tracking technologies.
  • Communication information: This includes the content and metadata of any messages you send or receive through our website or email, such as the subject, date, time, and recipient. We collect this information when you communicate with us or other users.

How we use your information

  • To provide our services: We use your personal information to provide our services, such as processing your orders, communicating with you, and personalizing your experience.
  • To improve our services: We use your personal information to understand how you use our website and identify areas for improvement.
  • To protect our services: We use your personal information to prevent and detect fraud, unauthorized access, and other illegal activities.
  • To comply with the law: We use your personal information to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

How we share your information

  • With our service providers: We share your personal information with our service providers to help us provide our services, such as processing your orders, delivering your books, and communicating with you.
  • With our partners: We share your personal information with our partners to help us provide our services, such as processing your payments and personalizing your experience.
  • With our affiliates: We share your personal information with our affiliates to help us provide our services, such as processing your orders and communicating with you.
  • With our users: We share your personal information with our users to help us provide our services, such as communicating with you.
  • With our successors: We share your personal information with our successors to help us provide our services, such as processing your orders and communicating with you.
  • With law enforcement: We share your personal information with law enforcement to help us comply with the law and protect our rights.

How we protect your information

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information and privacy. However, no security measure is perfect. Please keep your password safe and contact us if you notice any suspicious activity.
