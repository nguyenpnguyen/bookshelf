Grammar for IELTS

Diana Hopkins & Pauline Cullen

Grammar for IELTS by Diana Hopkins & Pauline Cullen is a book that provides complete coverage of the grammar needed for the IELTS test, the international English language testing system. The book also develops listening skills at the same time, as grammar and listening are often tested together in the exam. The book contains 25 units of grammar explanations and exercises, as well as a grammar reference section and an answer key. The book also includes an audio CD with authentic listening material from the test. The book is designed to be suitable for self-study or classroom use, and for students of different levels of ability. The book is suitable for anyone who wants to improve their grammar and listening skills for the IELTS test or for general purposes.

Language: English
