How To Write Essays & Assignments

Kathleen McMillan & Jonathan Weyers

How To Write Essays & Assignments by Kathleen McMillan & Jonathan Weyers is a book that provides practical and proven guidance on how to plan, research, write, and present academic essays and assignments. The book covers all the stages of the writing process, from understanding the question and finding sources, to structuring and editing the final draft. The book also offers tips and advice on how to avoid common mistakes, improve your grades, and manage your time effectively. The book is designed to be easy to use, with clear explanations, examples, checklists, and exercises. The book is suitable for students of any discipline and level of study, as well as anyone who wants to improve their writing skills for academic or professional purposes.

Language: English
